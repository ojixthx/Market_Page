Meet Our Team

ArchiSri is a student innovation project created by six passionate undergraduates from IIT. As aspiring technologists, we've combined our academic knowledge in AI, IoT, web development, and design to develop an innovative home design platform as part of our learning journey. This is our collaborative effort to apply classroom theory to real-world challenges.
